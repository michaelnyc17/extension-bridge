How It Works

  1. Click the Extension Bridge icon in your browser
  2. Click "Scan Extensions" to analyze your installed Chrome extensions
  3. Review matches categorized as Exact, Similar, or No Match
  4. Click "Compare" to see detailed side-by-side information
  5. Export results or click Firefox links to install alternatives
